About us

OUR VISION

Thriving home care services is a family owned and operated organization dedicated to providing quality care and advocacy for individuals and families transitioning care needs into the comfort of their own home. We thrive on supplying accountable, trustworthy, patient centered care.

OUR MISSION

We thrive everyday to provide the most reliable and compassionate care to our clients. THCS is devoted to establishing client-caregiver relationships with trust and accountability . Our unique and highly qualified staff are committed to fitting your needs. We believe with consistent education and training for our caregivers and incorporating a unique plan of care for our clients, THCS will be the ideal home care service of choice. Its your care, the way you want it, and we provide it. Let us show you how.

We provide free consultation services to Philadelphia,Chester, Bucks, Delaware, and Montgomery county .

​

Our experienced nurses are here to customize an optimize services for your specific needs with a detailed home assessment.

Our direct care workers are fully trained to implement and follow the plan of care for each client.

​

It Is your right to select your direct care worker whether it be from our existing HHA team or a family member of your choice.
